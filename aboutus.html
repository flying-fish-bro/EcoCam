About EcoCurrent

We're a team of engineers, ecologists, and dreamers on a mission to make clean energy accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Founded in 2019, EcoCurrent was born out of a frustration: clean energy was too expensive, too complicated, and too exclusive. We set out to change that.

We believe that sustainable living shouldn't be a luxury. Our technology is designed from the ground up to be affordable, powerful, and dead simple to use — whether you're powering a single apartment or an entire factory.

Today, our systems power over 50,000 homes across 28 countries, saving over 1.2 million tons of CO₂ annually. And we're just getting started.

🌍
"Clean energy isn't the future — it's already here. We're just making it everyone's."
