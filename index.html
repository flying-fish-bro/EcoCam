🌿 Wildlife Camera Tech

See every creature.
Miss nothing wild.

Wildlife moves fast and doesn't wait. EcoCam is a solar-powered, AI-enhanced trail camera built for researchers, rangers, and nature enthusiasts who need every sighting — day or night, storm or shine.

Explore EcoCam Our Story

Why EcoCam?

Most trail cameras were built for hunters. EcoCam was built for the planet — technology that respects animals, researchers, and the ecosystems they share.

☀️

Solar Powered, Forever

No more hauling battery packs through the backcountry. EcoCam's integrated solar array charges a 12,000mAh battery during daylight — deploy for months without a return trip.

🦎

AI Species ID On-Device

EcoCam's custom neural engine identifies 2,200+ species in under 200ms — no cloud, no signal required. Get Latin names, confidence scores, and instant alerts for species you care about.

📡

Satellite-Connected

The Iridium satellite modem sends photo alerts from anywhere on Earth. Amazon, Arctic, or the middle of the ocean — your sightings reach you in real time.

🌧️

Built for Brutal Conditions

IP68 waterproof and MIL-STD-810H certified. EcoCam survives monsoons, sub-zero nights, and the occasional curious bear. Rated from −40°C to +70°C.

👁️

No-Glow Infrared

940nm LEDs are completely invisible to animals. No red flash, no startle — just silent, undetectable observation that never alters the behavior you're studying.

♻️

Circular & Carbon-Neutral

Manufactured carbon-neutral. Every component is field-replaceable, and at end of life we recycle 94% of materials. EcoCam is built to last a decade.
