Our Story

Built by naturalists,
for the planet.

EcoCam started in a rainforest research camp in 2019, when our founders got tired of hauling battery packs through the jungle and missing shots because of failing hardware.

Our Mission

Why we exist — and why it matters.

We started EcoCam because documenting wildlife shouldn't require choosing between scientific rigor and environmental impact. Traditional camera traps are battery-hungry, plastic-heavy, and designed with the wrong priorities.

Our mission is to build tools that let researchers, rangers, and passionate citizens understand ecosystems more deeply, without disturbing them. Every EcoCam is manufactured carbon-neutral, built to last a decade, and designed to leave no trace on the environments it documents.

We believe the next era of wildlife science will be powered by technology that is as respectful of nature as the scientists who study it. EcoCam is that technology.

What We Stand For

The values baked into every camera we ship.

🌱

Leave No Trace

No-glow IR, silent shutters, and bio-compatible housing materials. The animals should never know we're there.

♻️

Circular Design

Every component is field-replaceable. When a camera reaches end of life, we take it back and recycle 94% of its materials.

🔬

Open Science

All anonymized sighting data is shared freely with the global research community via our open API.

🤝

Community First

10% of profits fund camera grants for indigenous-led conservation groups in biodiversity hotspots.
